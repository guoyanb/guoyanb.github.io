Yanbu Guo, Ph.D. He received the Ph.D. degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering, Yunnan University, Kunming, China, in 2021, and received a B.S. degree from College of Mathematics and Information Science, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, zhengzhou, China, in 2016. He is currently a Lecturer at the College of Software Engineering, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, Zhengzhou, China. His interests specifically focus on the following research topics:

  • Neural Networks : Graph/Bayesian/Quantum Neural Networks
  • Biomedical Informatics: Biomolecule–disease Associations/Drug Repositioning
  • Image Processing: Image Denoising/Enhancement/Fusion
