Yanbu Guo received the Ph.D. degree from Yunnan University, Kunming, China, and received a B.S. degree from Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, Zhengzhou, China. He is currently a Lecturer with College of Software Engineering, Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, Zhengzhou, China, and also a postdoctoral fellow with School of Automation, Southeast University, Nanjing, China. The interests mainly focus on the following research topics:

  • Neural Networks : Graph/Bayesian/Quantum Neural Networks
  • Biomedical Informatics: Biomolecule–disease Associations/Drug Repositioning
  • Image Processing: Image Denoising/Enhancement/Fusion
